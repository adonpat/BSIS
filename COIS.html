What is BSIS?

  • BSIS or Bachelor of Science in Information Systems focuses on the integration of technology, people, and business processes.
  • It prepares students to analyze, design, and manage information systems for organizations.
  • Differences:
    • IS – Focuses on business solutions using technology.
    • IT – Concentrates on hardware, networking, and system maintenance.
    • CS – Emphasizes programming, algorithms, and software development.
